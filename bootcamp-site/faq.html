Everything you need to know about admissions, tuition, and the residency program.

01. Admissions

We expect basic computer literacy. You don't need to know how to code, but you should be comfortable using a computer, typing, and managing files. We provide a 40-hour pre-work module that must be completed before Day 1.
We accept approximately 12% of applicants. We look for problem-solving ability, grit, and communication skills rather than prior coding knowledge.

02. The Program

Full-time: Mon-Fri, 9am - 6pm (EST). Expect 10-15 hours of homework per week.
Part-time: Mon-Thu, 6pm - 9pm (EST) + Sat 10am - 3pm. Expect 10-15 hours of homework per week.
We are currently 100% remote/live-online. We use Discord for communication, Zoom for lectures, and VS Code Live Share for pair programming.
You'll master modern technologies including Node.js, React, PostgreSQL, Docker, AWS, and more. We focus on production-grade tools and best practices used by top tech companies. The curriculum also covers system design, algorithms, and distributed systems architecture.

03. Tuition & ISA

With our Income Share Agreement (ISA), you pay $0 upfront. Once you're hired and earning at least $60,000/year, you pay 10% of your income for 24 months. Cap is $24,000.
Yes. If you choose to pay upfront, the tuition is $16,000 flat.
Our career support team works with you until you land a job. We provide ongoing interview prep, resume reviews, and direct referrals to our hiring partner network. With a 94% placement rate, most graduates find positions within 3-6 months of graduation. If you're on an ISA, you only start paying once you're earning over $60k/year.
